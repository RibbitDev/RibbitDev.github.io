About Me

I'm a Bachelor of Information Technology student majoring in Artificial Intelligence, passionate about creating AI-driven solutions that enhance accessibility, learning, and productivity. My goal is to design intuitive and user-friendly applications that empower individuals, especially in education and assistive technology.

With a strong interest in human-centred AI, I enjoy exploring the intersection of machine learning, neural networks, and adaptive algorithms to build impactful and efficient AI systems. My research interests include grokking, circuit efficiency, and information-theoretic methods for optimising neural networks, pushing the boundaries of AI efficiency and generalisation.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills in machine learning, neural networks, and AI-driven design, working on projects that bridge innovation with real-world impact. I'm always eager to collaborate on forward-thinking AI initiatives that make technology more accessible and effective.

Let's connect! I'd love to discuss AI, design, and innovative solutions that shape the future.
